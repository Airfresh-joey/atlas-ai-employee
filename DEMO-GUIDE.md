## What You're Demoing

ATLAS is a full-stack AI sales employee that:
1. **Learns about your company** - Chat to upload docs and define who you are
2. **Understands your ICP** - Conversational targeting setup
3. **Researches prospects** - Finds companies matching your criteria
4. **Generates personalized landing pages** - Custom pages for each prospect
5. **Tracks everything** - Live stats on prospects, emails, responses, meetings

## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How does it know about our company?**
A: You upload docs or paste text - ATLAS learns your services, differentiators, and value props.

**Q: Where do the prospects come from?**
A: In production, it connects to data sources like LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Apollo, or your CRM.

**Q: Can it really send emails?**
A: Yes - connects to your Gmail/Outlook. You approve templates first, then it runs campaigns.

**Q: What about landing page hosting?**
A: Pages deploy to your custom domain (e.g., go.airfreshmarketing.com/[prospect])

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Less than one SDR salary. Think of it as hiring your best salesperson, but they never sleep.

**Q: Can it integrate with our CRM?**
A: Yes - HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive. Syncs prospects, activities, and meetings.
