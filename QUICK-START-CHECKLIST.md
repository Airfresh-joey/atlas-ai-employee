### Email Deliverability

‚úÖ Start with 20 emails/day, ramp to 100/day  
‚úÖ Use AI personalization (not templates)

‚úÖ Use HeyReach (not direct automation)  
‚úÖ Limit to 20-30 actions/day per account  
‚úÖ Rotate between multiple accounts

### Content Quality
‚úÖ Feed AI rich context (prospect research)  
‚úÖ A/B test different approaches  
‚úÖ Review first 50 messages manually  
‚úÖ Iterate based on response rates

## üö® Common Pitfalls to Avoid

‚ùå **Sending too many emails too fast** ‚Üí Spam folder  
‚úÖ Warm up gradually over 2-4 weeks

‚ùå **Using same LinkedIn account for everything** ‚Üí Account ban  
‚úÖ Rotate between 5-10 accounts

‚ùå **Generic AI-generated emails** ‚Üí Low response rates  
‚úÖ Feed AI detailed prospect research

‚ùå **Ignoring replies** ‚Üí Wasted leads  
‚úÖ Set up reply detection + routing

‚ùå **No landing page tracking** ‚Üí Can't optimize  
‚úÖ Track everything (views, time, clicks)
